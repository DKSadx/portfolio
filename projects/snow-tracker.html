Snow Tracker

React native app that tracks the weather for two biggest ski resorts in Bosnia&Herzegovina. It also includes maps of the resorts.

GitHub →

Snow Tracker is a simple and easy-to-use Android/iOS app made with React Native. The app tracks the daily and hourly weather for the two biggest ski resorts in Bosnia & Herzegovina.
It calculates whether the ski conditions are good, moderate, or bad based on factors such as temperature, wind speed, precipitation, etc. Snow Tracker also offers official maps for the resorts. Weather data is sourced from the DarkSky API.
